Username Rules by Platform (Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube & More)

Choosing a username may seem simple, but each platform has its own rules and limitations. If your username is rejected or already taken, it’s often because it doesn’t meet platform-specific requirements.

This guide summarizes the most important username rules across major platforms so you can choose a valid and consistent handle.

Instagram Username Rules

Instagram usernames can contain letters, numbers, periods, and underscores. They must be between 1 and 30 characters long and cannot contain spaces.

If your desired username is taken, you may need to try variations or add modifiers such as prefixes or suffixes.

TikTok Username Rules

TikTok usernames must be between 2 and 24 characters long. They can include letters, numbers, underscores, and periods.

Usernames must be unique, and many short or common names are already taken due to platform popularity.

X (Twitter) Username Rules

X (formerly Twitter) usernames must be between 4 and 15 characters long. They can contain only letters, numbers, and underscores. No spaces are allowed.

Because the 15-character limit is quite strict, you may need to abbreviate your brand name if it's too long.

YouTube Username Rules

YouTube uses handles (formatted like @username) tied directly to a channel URL. Handles can include letters, numbers, underscores, hyphens, and periods. Range varies based on availability rules.

YouTube handles are unique across the entire platform, making it easier for creators to establish a distinct presence and help viewers find them.

Reddit Username Rules

Reddit usernames must be between 3 and 20 characters long. They can contain letters, numbers, hyphens, and underscores.

Reddit usernames cannot begin or end with a hyphen or underscore. Remember that Reddit account names are permanent and cannot be changed later.

GitHub Username Rules

GitHub usernames can be up to 39 characters long and must contain only alphanumeric characters or single hyphens.

You cannot have multiple consecutive hyphens, and the username cannot begin or end with a hyphen. Since GitHub is a professional platform, a clean variation of your real name or brand is highly recommended.

Common Username Issues

Why is my username taken but no account exists?

Some platforms reserve usernames even after accounts are deleted or inactive.

Why does one platform accept my username but another does not?

Each platform has different rules for length and allowed characters, so a valid username on one platform may not be valid on another.

Tips for Choosing a Good Username

  • Keep it short and easy to remember
  • Avoid complex symbols
  • Stay consistent across platforms
  • Check availability before committing
