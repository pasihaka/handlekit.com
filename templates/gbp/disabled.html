Understanding a Disabled Profile

What a disabled status may mean and how to prepare your evidence.

Important: This is preparation guidance, not a diagnosis or a guarantee of reinstatement.

Suspended vs. Disabled

A "Disabled" status on your business profile generally indicates an issue related to the account holder or the authenticity of the business entity itself. This is often more complex than a standard policy suspension.

What to Check First

  • Ownership conflicts (another user claims the same profile).
  • Lack of a primary business license or proper articles of incorporation.
  • Issues with the Google Account's standing or security flags.

Appeal Preparation

If you genuinely operate a valid local business, documentation like state business registration and matching utility bills are often reviewed during your appeal.
