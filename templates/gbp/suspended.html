Understanding a Suspended Profile

What a suspension means and how to prepare for reinstatement.

Important: This is preparation guidance, not a diagnosis or a guarantee of reinstatement.

What Suspension Means

Your listing is no longer visible on Google Maps and Search. A suspension means Google has flagged your profile for a potential policy violation or quality concern.

Common Issue Patterns

  • Address eligibility format problems may be one possible cause.
  • Lack of verifiable permanent signage is commonly checked for storefronts.
  • Recent sweeping edits to core business data can trigger reviews.

Evidence Gathering Before Appeal

Documentation that proves your entity and your connection to the specific location is usually reviewed. See our Evidence Checklist.

Official Next Steps

It is recommended to submit an appeal with solid proof. Use the Official Appeals Tool.
