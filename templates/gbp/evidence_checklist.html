The Evidence Checklist

What is commonly reviewed when guidelines are enforced.

Important: This is preparation guidance, not a diagnosis or a guarantee of reinstatement.

Storefront Evidence Patterns

  • Permanent exterior signage showing business name accurately.
  • Street view wide-angles showing the entrance and immediately adjacent businesses.
  • Interior workspace showing staffing, tools, or products.

Service-Area Evidence Patterns

  • Branded vehicles (wrap, magnet, or distinct decals) featuring contact info.
  • Tools of the trade stored at the registered base address.

Ownership & Entity Proof (For All)

  • Government-issued business license or corporate articles matching your exact registered name.
  • At least one utility bill explicitly listing the business entity and address.

Official Next Steps

Once your evidence is aggregated into a single PDF or clear folder, submit it via the Official Appeals Tool.
