Appeal Denied: Next Steps

How to reorganize after your initial evidence was rejected.

Important: This is preparation guidance, not a diagnosis or a guarantee of reinstatement.

What to Review Before Resubmitting Evidence

  • Review whether your submitted documentation had name mismatches.
  • Check if the phone number provided is actively tracked down to your business entity.

What Not To Do

  • Do not submit the exact same limited evidence again if it already failed.

Evidence Prep Checklist

If you intend to try support escalation or a further appeal, gather well-organized proof of your relationship to the business and location. See the evidence checklist for guidance.

Official Next Steps

There is no direct secondary appeal button. The next step often involves working directly through Google Business Profile Support.
