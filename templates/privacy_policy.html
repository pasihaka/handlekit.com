Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

HandleKit is built on a simple belief: your data is yours. This policy explains honestly and plainly what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have. We've written this to be readable, not to obscure anything in legal jargon.

1. Information We Collect

HandleKit is a "Privacy First" platform. The vast majority of tools, Password Generator, Word Counter, Text Case Converter, JSON Formatter, URL Encoder, Base64 Encoder, Hash Generator, Color Picker, and Unit Converter, run entirely within your browser using JavaScript. Any data you enter into these tools (text, passwords, JSON, URLs, etc.) is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

For tools that involve server interaction (Username Checker, Image Optimizer, QR Generator), only the minimum necessary data is sent to perform the requested function. For example, the Username Checker sends the username string to check availability on social platforms; the Image Optimizer receives your image file to compress it. This data is used solely to perform the requested function and is not stored or logged in a personally identifiable way after the request is completed.

2. Cookies and Advertisements

We use third-party advertising companies, including Google AdSense, to display ads when you visit our website. These companies may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons and pixel tags) to serve advertisements based on your browsing history on this and other websites.

Google's advertising cookies (DSID, IDE) enable personalised ad targeting based on browsing behaviour. You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time by visiting Google's Ad Settings, or by using the NAI opt-out tool. You may also use browser extensions that block advertising cookies entirely. Our site does not share personal data with advertisers beyond what is necessary for ad serving.

3. Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand which tools are most popular and how users navigate the site (for example, which pages are visited most often or where users exit). This helps us improve HandleKit and prioritise new features. Any analytics service we use is configured to anonymise IP addresses and not to share raw data with third parties beyond what is necessary for aggregate, non-personally-identifiable reporting.

4. Data Retention

We do not maintain a persistent database of user activity, tool inputs, or search queries. Server-side operations, such as image optimization, generate temporary files that are automatically deleted within minutes of the optimized file being delivered to you. We do not retain logs of individual requests in a form that could be used to identify a specific person.

5. External Links

Some HandleKit tools link to, or check availability on, external services (including GitHub, Reddit, Twitter/X, Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube). These are third-party services with their own independent privacy policies and data practices. HandleKit has no control over their content or practices. We encourage you to review the privacy policy of any third-party service you visit through links on our site. Our linking to a service does not imply endorsement of its privacy practices.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing of, and object to the processing of your personal data. You also have the right to data portability.

Because HandleKit is designed to collect minimal personal data, and most tools process data purely locally on your device, there is typically very little data to exercise these rights against. However, if you have any requests or questions, please contact us using the details below.

California residents have similar rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected and the right to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

HandleKit is not directed at children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. When we make significant changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of HandleKit after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Your Consent

By using our website, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your data rights, or have a concern about how your information is handled, please contact us at contacthandlekit@gmail.com. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
