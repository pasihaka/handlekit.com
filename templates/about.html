About HandleKit

HandleKit is a free, privacy-first toolbox for developers and digital creators. No sign-ups, no paywalls, just fast, reliable utilities that work right in your browser.

The project was built out of a simple frustration: too many essential tools are buried behind slow, ad-heavy, or subscription-gated websites. HandleKit puts them all in one clean, high-performance interface.

Who is behind HandleKit?

HandleKit is an independent project built and maintained by a solo developer with a focus on creating fast, privacy-first tools for everyday use.

The goal is simple: provide high-quality utilities that work instantly in the browser, without unnecessary tracking, paywalls, or complexity.

Many online tools today are slow, cluttered with ads, or require accounts to function. HandleKit is designed as a clean alternative, tools that just work when you need them.

The project is continuously improved based on user feedback, with new tools and features added over time.
