How Many Pages is 1000 Words?

The exact number of pages depends heavily on the font family, font size, margins, and spacing. However, the standard academic and publishing rule of thumb is:

  • Single Spaced: ~500 words per page
  • Double Spaced: ~250 words per page

This means a 1000 word essay is roughly 2 pages single-spaced, or 4 pages double-spaced (using standard 1-inch margins and a 12pt Arial or Times New Roman font). Using wider margins or larger fonts will significantly reduce the number of words per page.

Speech Speaking Times

The average adult speaks at roughly 130 words per minute in a presentation setting. Rushing causes you to stumble, so pacing is key!

  • 1 minute speech: ~130 words
  • 3 minute speech: ~390 words
  • 5 minute speech: ~650 words
  • 10 minute speech: ~1300 words

Common Word Count Targets

  • High School Essay: 1,000 - 1,500 words
  • College Term Paper: 2,000 - 3,000 words
  • Short Story: 3,000 - 7,000 words
  • Novella: 10,000 - 40,000 words
  • Novel: 70,000 - 120,000 words
