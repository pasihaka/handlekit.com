How Many Pages is My Word Count?

The number of pages your text will fill depends on the document size, font family, font size, margins, and spacing. While a standard A4 page is the global baseline, professional authors and legal teams often require more specific estimates.

  • A4 & US Letter: The standard for essays and business reports (~500 words per page).
  • Standard Book (6" x 9"): The most common trim size for trade paperbacks (~300 words per page).
  • Legal: Used for contracts and legal filings (~600 words per page).
  • A5: Ideal for brochures, booklets, and small journals (~250 words per page).

Using our converter, you can toggle between these common industry standards to get the most accurate page count for your specific project, whether it's a 1000-word essay or a 50,000-word novel.

How accurate is the page count?

The calculation is based on standard font sizing (12pt) and average margin settings. For Single Spacing, we estimate about 500 words per page, and for Double Spacing, it's approximately 275 words. Individual document formatting will vary slightly.

What is the "Standard Book" size?

The "Standard Book" option uses a 6" x 9" format (15.24 x 22.86 cm), which is the most common size for trade paperbacks. This is an essential reference if you are planning to self-publish a book or a novella.

Speech Speaking Times

The average adult speaks at roughly 130 words per minute in a presentation setting. Rushing causes you to stumble, so pacing is key!

  • 1 minute speech: ~130 words
  • 3 minute speech: ~390 words
  • 5 minute speech: ~650 words
  • 10 minute speech: ~1300 words

Common Word Count Targets

  • High School Essay: 1,000 - 1,500 words
  • College Term Paper: 2,000 - 3,000 words
  • Short Story: 3,000 - 7,000 words
  • Novella: 10,000 - 40,000 words
  • Novel: 70,000 - 120,000 words
